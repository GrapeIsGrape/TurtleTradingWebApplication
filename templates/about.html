Multiple Signals

When multiple breakouts fire on the same day, use this quick ranking checklist to make fast, disciplined choices.

1) Sector Filter First Highest Priority

If you already have an open trade in that GICS sector, skip the new setup. This alone removes most over-concentration risk.

2) Volatility Rank (Highest First)

Choose the ticker with the biggest 20-day ATR. Bigger expected movement can translate into better reward when the breakout follows through.

3) Momentum Strength
  • Prefer the one trading farther above its 200-day moving average.
  • Or compare breakout force: pick the one that cleared the 55-day high by a wider margin.
4) Liquidity Tie-Breaker

If candidates are still close, pick the one with higher average daily dollar volume for smoother fills and tighter spreads.

5) Still Tied?

Take the first symbol in your scan order (FIFO), or randomize. Do not overthink the final tie.
